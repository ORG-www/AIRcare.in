ABOUT US

  Air Care Electronics was established in 1967 by Shri Jagdish Narayan Rastogi and were pioneers in Lucknow in assembling of Air Conditioners. Soon, they expanded their product portfolio to include refrigerators, washing machines, LED TV and other electronic goods. 50 years down the line.
    In 2017, his grandson Anugrah Rastogi joined the well established world of Air Care Electronics. Anugrah identified a great opportunity in the fast growing Lucknow market which showed trend of contemporary housing, new commercial complexes and developing infrastructure.
All these premises required air conditioning and there were limited players that provided holistic central  air conditioning solutions.

The idea clicked and within 2 years, Air Care Electronics have become a first choice for central air conditioning solutions across Lucknow. ACE has exclusive pan India tie-ups for VRV applications. The company is extremely particular about working with the best market players to ensure customer satisfaction.
  ACE, is first in the industry to get Fire retardant outdoor insulation for extra protection, Spiral oval ducts, Detailed terrace drawings of outdoor placement and Fire retardant canvass cloth between duct and mouth piece of indoor.
ACE ensures that their clients not only get the best air conditioners but also ensure that they mix well with aesthetics of the premises. This makes them a popular choice in food and beverage industry.
